Our Mission

Sona aims to be the market leader in Transformers & Coils business by becoming preferred supplier through technological operational and service excellence. The vision of SONA LED is to evolve in one of the key players in the research, development and production of LED luminaires , always within the framework of sustainable growth.

Our Core Values

  • To offer our clients complete solutions with high-end products.

  • To provide our customers excellent products with a cost efficiency and accuracy.
